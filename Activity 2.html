ACTIVITY 2:
Tell Me About Your Favorite Application


Before choosing my favorite app, I had to double-check my phone and go through all my apps before I made my decision. Being a person who loves to make and listen to music, I concluded that my favorite app is the Shazam app. I’ve been using the app for almost 5 years now. I think all of us have had moments where we hear a very good song and then try to remember the lyrics so we can google it and hopefully find the title.

The Shazam app helps you find out the name of the song that’s being played with just one tap. All you have to do is tap the Shazam button, then hold your device towards where the music is coming from and then it immediately looks for the song. The result will instantly show as long as you’re connected to the internet but when you aren’t connected, it saves the captured song(s) on offline mode and then searches for it later when you’re connected. Another good feature of the app is the history. You can look at a list of all the songs you’ve “Shazamed” for future reference. The Shazam app also helps you discover new music and videos at the Discover tab. I love the Shazam app because I’m a musician, and sometimes I hear songs that I want to learn and practice when I get home. I will definitely recommend this app to my friends.

Another one of my favorite apps is Waze. Waze is a community-driven GPS and navigational app that helps users find the best routes while driving. I have been using this since I was 17; the age I started driving. I love this app because I have a very poor sense of direction. When I drive, I’m not good at remembering landmarks so even if I’ve been to a certain place once or twice, I still need the app so that I won’t get lost. One key feature of Waze is notifying other drivers when there are road hazards. When you see something that might hinder other drivers, you can tap different options such as; accidents, potholes, and even policemen (useful when you’re coding hehe). Waze takes all these things into account and then finds another route for the user. Waze can also help users locate for parking around their destination which is very useful around big cities like BGC or Makati. Waze is a tool that I will continue to use and recommend to different people.
