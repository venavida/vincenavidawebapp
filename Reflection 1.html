REFLECTION 1:
1st Week of USERDES Class

Our first week was fun for me, probably because we haven’t started coding yet HAHA. Kidding aside, I had fun because I consider myself to be a right-brained person so I enjoy creativity. I learned this past week that before designing something in general, there are a lot of things to consider such as its’ purpose, the people using it, their preferences, and many more. Instead of thinking about what I like or what I want, I need to think about what others want as well. Designing is complicated and simple at the same time, I know it seems like it doesn’t make sense, but the designing process is full of careful steps that need to be followed and yet designing could be just using your imagination and making it real.

The atmosphere in our classroom is very nice. I think it has a lot to do with Ms. Jen being lively and the fact that she’s younger and more engaging compared to our other professors. I think my classmates like that better and respond in a positive way. For me, I immediately approved of Ms. Jen because she watches anime. I hope that one of these days we’ll be able to play a game that involves our topic.

In conclusion, I think we’ve gotten off to a good start. We’ve talked about what design is and some of the aspects surrounding it. We’ve got a good environment, I just think that everyone, including myself, is still trying to get a good grasp of the topic so that we can speak more in class. I know that all of my blockmates can contribute to our class. I look forward to our future meetings, except the programming part.
